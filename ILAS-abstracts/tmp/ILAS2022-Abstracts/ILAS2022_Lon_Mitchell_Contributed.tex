% The abstract  ***no more than one page***

We extend recent results of Alfakih~\cite{A}, who constructed Colin de Verdi\`ere matrices for complements of penny graphs from Euclidean distance matrices, by interpreting them using the sphere representations of Kotlov, Lov\'{a}sz, and Vempala~\cite{B}.   Our results apply to complements of contact graphs of unit spheres in arbitrary dimension.
