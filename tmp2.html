Research

My area of research is Numerical Analysis and Computational Mathematics. I am particularly interested in the design and analysis of novel discretizations and/or linear solvers for singularly perturbed differential equations, and in the application of novel methods in numerical modelling. For details on interests, publications and projects, see my research page.

There are also links to my publications on Google Scholar, and my NUI Galway institutional website.

If you share these interests, and would like join our group as a postdoctoral, graduate, or undergraduate researcher, please contact me to discuss possible sources of funding.
