UPDATE (05/04/2017): the Book of Abstracts is now available.

Abstracts

Each speaker is invited to submit an abstract of his/her talk. A collection of (extended) abstracts will be published here by the beginning of the workshop.

Abstracts should be submitted as LaTeX files, whose length including figures and references should not exceed 2 pages. The LaTeX files to prepare your abstract are:

  • the LaTeX document-class file layers17.cls,
  • a sample abstract: YourName.tex.
(Right click and choose Save Link As...).

Each contributed talk should not exceed 20 minutes (including a short discussion). A computer and data-projector will be available, as well as a white board and over-head projector.

The deadline for submitting an abstract is Friday, 24 February 2017.
