Euclidean Distance Matrices and the Colin de Verdière Number of a Graph

Lon Mitchell

We extend recent results of Alfakih , who constructed Colin de Verdière matrices for complements of penny graphs from Euclidean distance matrices, by interpreting them using the sphere representations of Kotlov, Lovász, and Vempala . Our results apply to complements of contact graphs of unit spheres in arbitrary dimension.
