Here we show the numerical solution to $$ u_t - \varepsilon^2 \Delta u + r(x,y)u = f(x,y,t) $$ where the spacial domain is \( \Omega:=(0,1)^2 \), and the PDE's domain is \( (x,y,t) \in \Omega \times(0,1] \). Boundary conditions: \( u(\cdot,\cdot,t)|_{\partial \Omega}=0 \), and initial condition us \( u(x,y,0)\equiv 0\). For the specific example is described in the Overleaf Project.
