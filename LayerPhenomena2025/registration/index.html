Registration and Abstracts

The Workshop is free, but registration is required.

To register your interest in participating, please complete this form, or send an email to Niall (Niall.Madden@UniversityOfGalway.ie) with your name and affiliation, and information on if you wish to give a talk.

The deadline for registration will be posted here 6 months before the workshop.

Tea and coffee will be provided during breaks. Lunch, the conference dinner, travel, accommodation, etc., are at the participants' expense.
