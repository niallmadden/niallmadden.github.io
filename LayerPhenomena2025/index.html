Layer Phenomena 2025

21st Workshop on Numerical Methods for Problems with Layer Phenomena

24th and 25th of April 2025

University of Galway, Ireland

This two-day workshop will bring together researchers with interests in problems whose solutions exhibit layer phenomena, such as boundary and interior layers in fluid flow, and other applications. The main focus is on the design, analysis and application of numerical methods for these problems, but contributions on related topics, such as applications and asymptotic methods, are warmly welcomed.

Details

  • The workshop runs from the morning of Thursday, 24 April, and finishes by lunchtime on Friday, 25 April
  • All talks are contributed, and speakers will have approximately 25 minutes each (depending on the number of talks).

Location and travel

The workshop is hosted by the School of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences, University of Galway, Ireland. Talks will take place in AC201, in the Arts and Science Concourse.
