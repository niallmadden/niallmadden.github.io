Layer Phenomena 2025

Registration and Abstracts

Registration

  • The Workshop is free, but registration is required.
  • Please register by filling out this Registration form, or by sending an email to Niall.Madden@UniversityOfGalway.ie with your name and affiliation, stating if you would like to give a talk, and whether you agree to having your name and affiliation included in the list of of participants on the website.
  • The deadline for registration is Friday, 28th March 2025.
  • Tea and coffee will be provided during breaks, thanks to support from the Irish Mathematical Society, and the School of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences, University of Galway. Lunch, the conference dinner, travel, accommodation, etc., are at the participants' expense; unfortunately, we do not have any funds to support participants' expenses.

Abstract Submission

  • Submission deadline: Friday 28 March 2025.
  • Abstracts should be submitted by email to Niall.Madden@UniversityOfGalway.ie
  • Please use the $\LaTeX$ template provided, and follow instructions in that file. Or you can submit your abstract as plain text in your email, including
    1. Title of presentation
    2. Speaker's name
    3. Speaker's affiliation
    4. Abstract in plain text, with $\LaTeX$ equations if needed.
