Layer Phenomena: 2025

24-25 April 2025

University of Galway, Ireland

The "Layer Phenomena Workshop" (also known as the "Limerick Workshop") has been running since 2002, skipping occasional years to avoid clashes with the BAIL Conference, or a pandemic
