The School of Mathematics, Statistics and Applied Mathematics at NUI Galway is hosting a four day undergraduate modelling workshop from June 13th to 16th. The format will be loosely based on the Mathematical Contest in Modelling, with training and small group study sessions provided by lecturers and postgraduate students of the School of Mathematics.

Registration is free, and some financial support may be available to cover accommodation and living expenses.

The workshop is primarily aimed at students in their penultimate year of study, but applications are welcome from all interested undergraduates. Interested students are invited to fill out the application form below as soon as possible, and no later than Friday June 3rd.

General queries relating to the workshop should be directed to paul.greaney@nuigalway.ie.

The poster announcing the workshop is also available, as are the webpages of the 2014 and 2015 workshops.

Planned Activities

  • Crash training in numerical methods
  • Team work on a modelling problem
  • Presentation of results
  • Short talks on applied maths topics by lecturers
  • Research, from postgraduate students' point of view
